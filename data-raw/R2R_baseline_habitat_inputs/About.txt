The R2R baseline habtiat is generated by scaling SIT habtiat to account for new proposed or implemented projects. All projects are listed in the SalmonRecovery_Project_Catalog_FINAL_7.28.22_INTERNAL_worksesh1_EC_2-2-2023.xlsx. These projects are summarized in the R2R_project_catalog_summary.csv and suitability percentages are described for each habtiat type. This sumary tables is used to generate habitat.
